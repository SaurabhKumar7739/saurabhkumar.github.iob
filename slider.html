NAAC A+ Accredited Institute

NAAC – National Assessment & Accreditation Council is the country’s highest accreditation council set up by the Government of India. It is an autonomous organization under the Ministry of Human Resource Development and UGC (University Grants Commission). Only 4% of the total accredited educational institutes in India have been accredited with NAAC A+ grade. With NAAC A+, the institute is preferred in gaining funding for startups, favoured by international companies for placements, and it is easier to get admitted to a Foreign University for further studies.

Centre of Excellence

Marwadi University is granted the Centre of Excellence status by the Government of Gujarat. As defined by the State Government, the Centre of Excellence means that the College, University, or Private University are imparted with Quality Education, Path breaking Research, Quality Extension Services, Innovative Teaching Methodologies, High Employability and Entrepreneurship.

Placement Opportunities in MNCs

MU has a large, dedicated full-time Placement & Corporate Relations department that ensures deserving students get placed in top companies in reputable positions. We hosted over 480 companies (Year 2020-21) for their campus recruitment drive and created 10000+ job opportunities for our graduates. MU not only facilitates the student's placements in top corporate companies but also takes a Three Layers of Learning approach via strategic development programmes and comprehensive training programmes throughout the year to retrain the students in the various areas.

Admission Criteria

Under Graduate

We assess your academic performance, along with co-curricular and leadership involvement. Eligibility requires 10+2 completion or a Bachelor’s degree from a recognised board.

Post Graduate

Admission is primarily merit-based, though some PG courses require national entrance exam scores (CMAT, GATE) followed by counselling. Eligibility requires a Bachelor’s degree in a relevant field.

PHD

The basic eligibility for Ph.D. admission is a Postgraduate degree. Selection is based on merit from an entrance exam, followed by an interview.
